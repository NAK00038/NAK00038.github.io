Amélie

Director: Jean-Pierre Jeunet

Genre: Comedy

Year released: 2001

Awards: European Film Award
Fun fact: After the release of the movie, the name Amélie became more popular

Amélie is a beautiful story about a shy girl named Amélie who decides to help people find happiness.
The movie follows Amélie as she ends up on an adventure with relatives, neighbors and strangers.
Even small actions can change the world - this is the main message of the movie.
Amélie learns that there is beauty everywhere when you look hard enough
